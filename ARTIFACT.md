## What This Artifact Proves

The artifact establishes a **general viability-boundary theorem family**:

> For record-bearing recursively constrained systems, viable continuation requires sufficient constraint capacity, trace capacity, anchor fidelity, channel independence, and local-to-global compatibility relative to transition pressure and destabilizing load. When these deficits compound beyond admissible bounds, pathology, fragility, or collapse is unavoidable.

Domain instantiations (AGI, law, institutions, markets, biology, ecology, civilizations, etc.) are realized as interpretation bridges (Phase VII) and FrontierPrinciples (Phase VII+).
